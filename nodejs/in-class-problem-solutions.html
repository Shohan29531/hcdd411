Problems covered here

  • Search: ?contains=word
  • Timestamp filter: ?updatedAfter=TIMESTAMP
  • Sort: ?order=desc or ?order=asc
  • Limit: ?limit=5
  • Validation: bad limit or updatedAfter returns 400

Behavior summary

  • Search is case-insensitive.
  • Sorting uses updatedAt.
  • Limiting happens after sorting/filtering.
  • The returned shape remains a normal notes array.
